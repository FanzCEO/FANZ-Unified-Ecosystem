DaddyFanz brand kit (v1)

Core palette (dark-first UI)
	•	Dominant Blue (Primary): #00CFFF — the neon cyan glow from your logo, anchors CTAs/links
	•	Gold Drip (Secondary): #F5C237 — metallic accents for premium, wealth/power vibes
	•	Chain Steel (Accent): #7A7F89 — for dividers, borders, icons
	•	Ink (Bg): #0A0A0B — global background
	•	Brick Black (Surface): #121213 — card/modals (echoes brick wall)
	•	Snow (Text): #F5F7FA — primary copy
	•	Fog (Muted text): #9CA3AF — secondary/labels
	•	Success: #22C55E
	•	Warning: #F59E0B
	•	Error: #EF4444

DaddyFanz reads heavier and more industrial than Boy/Girl/Pup—so we lean on cold metal + glowing blue, with gold as a luxury drip.

⸻

Signature gradients
	•	Dominance: linear-gradient(135deg, #00CFFF 0%, #0047FF 60%, #F5C237 100%)
	•	Dungeon Glow: radial-gradient(1200px 600px at 60% -15%, rgba(0,207,255,0.16) 0%, #000 65%)
	•	Golden Edge: linear-gradient(90deg, #F5C237, #FFD97A)

⸻

Typography
	•	Display: Space Grotesk / Bebas Neue — sharp, masculine, compressed
	•	Body: Inter / Urbanist — clean, no-nonsense readability
	•	Weighting: heavy on headlines, light on body; hierarchy should scream “authority.”

⸻

UI mood
	•	Chains + bricks: subtle embossed chain motif in dividers, or background overlays at low opacity.
	•	Glow: neon outer glows (0 0 24px #00CFFF80) on CTAs, avatars, active states.
	•	Buttons = blocky, squared-off (6–8px radius max), feels harder than Pup/Girl.
	•	Motion: subtle pulsing glow (never bouncy—more commanding).

⸻

Component cues
	•	Primary button: Cyan fill → on hover Cyan→Gold gradient; text Snow; glow effect.
	•	Secondary button: Chain Steel outline; hover Steel fill 12%.
	•	Tabs: Active underline Cyan 3px glow; inactive = Fog.
	•	Cards: Brick Black with 1px Chain Steel border; top edge highlight in Gold Drip.
	•	Badges: Ranks (Alpha, Dom, Daddy Verified) in Cyan/Gold dual-tone.

⸻

Accessibility
	•	Use Snow for all text; Fog for secondary.
	•	Cyan and Gold both contrast well against Ink backgrounds—just avoid Cyan text on Gold.
	•	Focus rings: Cyan outer, Gold inner.
