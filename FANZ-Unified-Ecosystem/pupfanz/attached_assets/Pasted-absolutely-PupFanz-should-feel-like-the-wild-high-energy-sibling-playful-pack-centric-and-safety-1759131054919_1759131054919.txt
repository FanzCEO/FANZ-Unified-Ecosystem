PupFanz brand kit (v1)

Core palette (dark-first UI)
	•	PF Volt (Primary): #A3FF12 — hyper-neon lime for CTAs/active
	•	Cobalt (Secondary): #2563EB — cool depth for highlights & nav
	•	Tangerine (Accent): #FF7A00 — motion cues, alerts, fun badges
	•	Ink (Bg): #0A0A0B — global background
	•	Graphite (Surface): #121214 — cards, sheets, modals
	•	Snow (Text on dark): #F5F7FA — primary copy
	•	Mist (Muted text): #C9D0D6 — secondary/labels
	•	Success: #22C55E
	•	Warning: #F59E0B
	•	Error: #EF4444

Why: Volt Lime reads athletic + playful (fits “pup/pack” energy) and punches hard against dark UI. Cobalt keeps cohesion with the FUN network’s cyber vibe. Tangerine adds warmth and motion pop.

Signature gradients
	•	Pack Howl (hero/CTA): linear-gradient(135deg, #A3FF12 0%, #2563EB 60%, #00E5FF 100%)
	•	Trail Mix (hover wash): linear-gradient(90deg, #A3FF12, #FF7A00)
	•	Moon Yard (bg glow): radial-gradient(1100px 520px at 70% -8%, rgba(37,99,235,0.14) 0%, #000 60%)

Typography
	•	Display: Space Grotesk / Sora — bold, condensed tracking for howl-level headlines
	•	Body: Inter / Outfit — crisp, approachable, great at small sizes

UI mood & motion
	•	Shapes: rounded 12px radius (slightly “chew-toy” feel), beveled icon corners.
	•	Glows: soft outer glow on Volt (0 0 24px #A3FF1240).
	•	Motion: 140–180ms ease-out; micro-bounce on toggles; pack-join confetti using tiny paw/bolt glyphs.
	•	Iconography: minimalist paw/bolt/bone line icons; avoid cartoon clip-art.

Component cues
	•	Primary button: PF Volt fill → on hover “Trail Mix” gradient; text Snow; subtle glow.
	•	Secondary button: Cobalt outline; hover fill Cobalt 12%.
	•	Nav tabs: Graphite base; active tab underline 3px Volt; inactive underline 1px Cobalt 40%.
	•	Cards: Graphite with 1px border #26262A; top edge neon-lime hairline.
	•	Switches & sliders: track Cobalt; thumb Volt; on-change emits 80ms tangerine flash.
	•	Badges: “Verified Pup,” “Consent-Clear,” “Aftercare-Friendly” in Cobalt/Tangerine duotone.

Safety & inclusivity baked-in (visible UX)
	•	Consent status chip on profiles (defaults neutral; turns Success when all toggles set).
	•	Aftercare tag opt-in; boosts discovery in “Supportive pack” filter.
	•	Session boundaries micro-form uses Tangerine accents for edits; confirmation in Volt.

Accessibility guardrails
	•	Use Snow for all primary text.
	•	Volt on Ink/Graphite passes contrast for button labels when text is Snow; avoid Volt text on dark—use Cobalt/Snow instead.
	•	Minimum 14px body; 16px for interactive labels.
	•	Focus ring: 2px dotted Cobalt + 1px inner Volt.
