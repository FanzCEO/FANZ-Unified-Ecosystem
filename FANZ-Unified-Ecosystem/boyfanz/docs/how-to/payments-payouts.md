### "Chargeback Occurred"

**What is a chargeback?**
Fan disputes payment with their bank/card company.

**What happens:**
- Amount deducted from balance
- $15 chargeback fee applied
- Your account flagged
- Too many = account review

**How to prevent:**
- Deliver promised content
- Clear content descriptions
- Respond to fan questions
- Keep records/screenshots
- Good customer service

**If you get one:**
1. Review chargeback reason
2. Provide evidence (if legit sale)
3. Platform fights on your behalf
4. Win rate: 60-70% with good evidence

## üìã Tax Information

### U.S. Creators (1099)

**What you need to know:**

**Tax Forms:**
- Earn >$600/year: Receive 1099-NEC
- Provided by January 31st
- Report as self-employment income
- File Schedule C

**Estimated Taxes:**
- Pay quarterly (April, June, Sept, Jan)
- Use Form 1040-ES
- Avoid penalties
- Set aside 25-30%

**Deductions You Can Claim:**
- Equipment (camera, lights)
- Internet service
- Home office (if applicable)
- Props and costumes
- Software subscriptions
- Marketing expenses
- Professional services

---

### International Creators (W-8BEN)

**Tax treaty benefits:**

**W-8BEN Form:**
- Reduces U.S. tax withholding
- Required for non-U.S. creators
- Valid for 3 years
- Submit via dashboard

**Withholding Rates:**
- Without W-8BEN: 30% withheld
- With W-8BEN: 0-15% (varies by country)
- Check your country's tax treaty
- Significant savings!

**Your Country's Taxes:**
- Report all earnings
- Each country different
- Consult local tax professional
- Keep detailed records
