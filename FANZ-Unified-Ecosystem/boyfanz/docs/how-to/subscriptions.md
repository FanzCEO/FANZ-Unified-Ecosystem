**Pricing tiers:**

**Budget Tier ($5-9.99)**
- Great for starting out
- Low barrier to entry
- Higher subscriber count
- Volume-based earnings

**Standard Tier ($10-19.99)**
- Most popular range

- Established creators
- High-quality content
- Exclusive experiences
- More personalization
- Higher earning per fan

**VIP Tier ($35-50+)**
- Top-tier creators
- Ultra-exclusive content
- Personal attention
- Special perks included
- Maximum revenue per sub

### Free Trials

**Should you offer trials?**

**Pros:**
- Lower barrier to entry
- Higher initial conversions
- Let fans "try before they buy"
- Showcase your value
- Convert free users

**Cons:**
- Some won't convert to paid
- Immediate revenue loss
- Potential abuse
- Requires tracking

**Best practices:**
- 3-7 day trial (not too long)
- Require payment method
- Auto-converts to paid
- Clear communication
- One trial per user

**When to use trials:**
- Starting out (build base fast)
- Launching new tier
- Promotional campaigns
- Re-engaging old subs
- Special events

## üêõ Troubleshooting

### "Subscribers aren't converting"

**Possible issues:**
- Price too high (test lower)
- Unclear benefits (better description)
- Not enough free content (tease more)
- Profile incomplete (optimize)
- Poor quality previews (improve)

**Solutions:**
1. A/B test pricing
2. Rewrite subscription benefits
3. Post more free teasers
4. Complete profile 100%
5. Showcase best content

---

### "High cancellation rate"

**Common causes:**
- Inconsistent posting
- Low engagement
- Not meeting expectations
- Better options available
- Price vs value mismatch

**Fix it:**
1. Set and keep schedule
2. Interact more with subs
3. Deliver promised content
4. Add unique value
5. Survey departing subs

---

### "Payment failed for subscriber"

**What happens:**
- Card declined/expired
- Subscription paused
- Subscriber notified
- Grace period (3-7 days)
- Auto-canceled if not fixed

**What you do:**
- Platform handles it automatically
- Subscriber gets email reminder
- Access continues during grace period
- Removed if payment not updated
- You don't need to do anything
