Federal Compliance Statement

Fanz Unlimited Network LLC is fully compliant with Title 18 U.S.C. § 2257 and all related federal record-keeping requirements for producers of sexually explicit content. All models appearing on this website were at least 18 years of age when the content was created.

1. Legal Requirements Overview

Age Verification

All performers must provide government-issued photo identification proving they are at least 18 years of age before any content creation.

Record Keeping

Detailed records of all performers' identification documents must be maintained for a minimum of seven (7) years after content creation.

Record Inspection

Records must be available for inspection by authorized federal law enforcement officers during reasonable business hours.

Custodian Notice

The location of records and identity of the custodian must be clearly disclosed on all materials containing sexually explicit conduct.

2. FANZ Compliance Measures

Mandatory Identity Verification

Every creator must complete our comprehensive identity verification process before uploading any content. This includes:

  • Government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, or state ID)
  • Real-time selfie verification holding ID
  • Biometric verification to prevent fraud
  • Cross-reference with government databases where legally permissible

Comprehensive Record Keeping

We maintain detailed records including:

  • Digital copies of all government-issued identification
  • Verification timestamps and IP addresses
  • Content creation dates and locations
  • Performer consent documentation
  • Regular audit trails and compliance reviews

Secure Storage & Access

All compliance records are:

  • Stored in military-grade encrypted databases
  • Accessible only to authorized compliance personnel
  • Backed up redundantly across multiple secure locations
  • Protected with multi-factor authentication
  • Maintained for the legally required retention period

Ongoing Compliance Monitoring

Our compliance program includes:

  • Regular legal reviews and updates
  • Staff training on 2257 requirements
  • Automated compliance checking systems
  • Third-party compliance audits
  • Immediate response procedures for violations
