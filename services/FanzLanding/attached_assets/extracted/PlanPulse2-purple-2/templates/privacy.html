We prohibit anyone under the age of eighteen (18) from accessing the Site or using our Services. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you are under 18, you must not submit information to us and must immediately leave the Site. If we learn that we have collected information from someone under 18, we will delete that information immediately. Contact us at privacy@fanzunlimited.net if you believe we might have such information.
