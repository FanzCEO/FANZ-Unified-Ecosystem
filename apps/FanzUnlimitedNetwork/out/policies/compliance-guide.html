This guide is specifically for Content Stars (creators/performers) and Co-Stars (collaborators) on FANZ platforms. Whether you're just starting or are an established creator, this resource will help you understand legal requirements, avoid common pitfalls, and protect your business.

1. Getting Started: Account Setup & Verification

1.1 Creator Application Checklist

Before you can monetize, you must complete:

  • ‚úÖ Government ID Verification

    Valid passport, driver's license, or state ID. Must show your face, date of birth, and full legal name. You'll complete a real-time selfie + liveness check through VerifyMy.

  • ‚úÖ Tax Form Submission

2.1 Understanding Your Tax Obligations

You are an independent contractor, not an employee. This means:

  • ‚Ä¢ FANZ does NOT withhold income taxes from your earnings
  • ‚Ä¢ You are responsible for paying federal, state, and local taxes on your earnings
  • ‚Ä¢ You must file quarterly estimated taxes if you expect to owe $1,000+ annually
  • ‚Ä¢ You can deduct business expenses (equipment, props, internet, etc.) on Schedule C

3. Content Requirements & 2257 Compliance

3.1 Age Verification for Co-Stars

If you collaborate with anyone in your content (co-stars, models, performers), you MUST verify they are 18+ and maintain records:

  • Government ID Copy

    Photocopy or scanned image showing full name, date of birth, and photo. Keep securely for 7 years.

  • Model Release Form

    Signed consent from each co-star permitting use of their likeness. Download template from your creator dashboard.

  • Content Records

    Keep a list of which content features which co-stars. FANZ maintains platform-level 2257 records, but you should keep backup records.

3.2 Consent & Safe Words

For BDSM, fetish, or role-play content involving simulated non-consent:

  • ‚Ä¢ Document pre-scene consent conversations (video/written)
  • ‚Ä¢ Establish and use safe words/signals
  • ‚Ä¢ Include aftercare and check-ins for intense scenes
  • ‚Ä¢ Never upload content where consent was withdrawn or safe word was used

üö® Zero Tolerance Content

The following content will result in immediate permanent ban:

  • ‚Ä¢ Minors (anyone under 18) in any context
  • ‚Ä¢ Bestiality or animal abuse
  • ‚Ä¢ Non-consensual "revenge porn" or leaked content
  • ‚Ä¢ Content depicting actual violence, gore, or death
  • ‚Ä¢ Human trafficking or coerced participation

4. Intellectual Property & DMCA

4.1 Protecting Your Content

  • ‚Ä¢ You own your content. FANZ only receives a license to host/distribute it.
  • ‚Ä¢ Watermark your photos and videos with your creator name or platform logo
  • ‚Ä¢ Register your most valuable content with the U.S. Copyright Office ($65 per work or $85 per group)
  • ‚Ä¢ Use FANZ's DMCA takedown service to remove pirated content from other sites (free for creators)

4.2 Respecting Others' IP

Do NOT upload content that:

  • ‚Ä¢ Contains copyrighted music without a license (use royalty-free music instead)
  • ‚Ä¢ Features trademarked characters or brands without permission
  • ‚Ä¢ Includes clips from movies, TV shows, or other creators' content
  • ‚Ä¢ Uses someone else's photos/videos without their consent

DMCA Strike Policy

  • ‚Ä¢ 1st strike: Content removed, warning issued
  • ‚Ä¢ 2nd strike: 7-day suspension
  • ‚Ä¢ 3rd strike: Permanent account termination

5.2 Payout Schedule & Minimums

  • ‚Ä¢ Weekly payouts: Every Monday (minimum $20 via ACH)
  • ‚Ä¢ Monthly payouts: 1st of the month (minimum $20 via ACH)

Common reasons for payout holds:

  • ‚Ä¢ Incomplete tax forms (W-9/W-8BEN missing or expired)
  • ‚Ä¢ High chargeback rate (>1% of transactions)
  • ‚Ä¢ Pending policy violation investigation
  • ‚Ä¢ Banking information doesn't match verified ID name

6. Community Guidelines & Best Practices

6.1 Building a Sustainable Business

  • ‚Ä¢ Set realistic subscription prices ($5-$50/month is typical)
  • ‚Ä¢ Post consistently (3-5 times per week minimum)
  • ‚Ä¢ Engage with subscribers via DMs and comments
  • ‚Ä¢ Offer exclusive PPV content for top spenders
  • ‚Ä¢ Promote your FANZ page on social media (Twitter, Reddit, Instagram)

6.2 Ethical Subscriber Interactions

  • ‚Ä¢ Deliver promised content in a timely manner
  • ‚Ä¢ Don't make false promises to extract tips or subscriptions
  • ‚Ä¢ Disclose if content is pre-recorded vs. custom/personalized
  • ‚Ä¢ Respect subscriber privacy (don't leak DMs or payment info)
  • ‚Ä¢ Block/report abusive or harassing subscribers

6.3 Mental Health & Burnout Prevention

  • ‚Ä¢ Set boundaries on availability and response times
  • ‚Ä¢ Take breaks when needed (announce hiatuses to subscribers)
  • ‚Ä¢ Access FANZ's Healthcare Resources for mental health support
  • ‚Ä¢ Join creator communities (CreatorFanz Discord, subreddit, etc.) for peer support
