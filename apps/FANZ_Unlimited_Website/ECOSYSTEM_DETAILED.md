### FanzMediaCore
**Content Fortress**
Handles every piece of uploaded content with forensic precision.

**Processing Pipeline:**
1. Upload received
2. Real-time virus scanning
3. AI content moderation
4. **FanzForensics™ signature embedding** (invisible, forensic-grade digital fingerprint)
5. Multi-format transcoding
6. Watermarking (visible & invisible)
7. Sent to FanzDash for admin approval
8. Global CDN delivery

**FanzForensics™:**
Every piece of content gets an invisible, forensic-grade digital signature embedded at the binary level. If your content is stolen and posted elsewhere, FanzForensics can trace it back to the exact upload timestamp, user account, and device. Our AI scans the web 24/7, automatically issuing DMCA takedowns and protecting intellectual property with military precision.

**Security:**
- Forensic fingerprinting on ALL content
- Invisible watermark embedding
- Content integrity verification
- Piracy detection AI
- Automated DMCA enforcement
- Real-time content scanning

## Security Architecture

### Multi-Layered Defense
1. **Perimeter Security** - DDoS protection, WAF, rate limiting
2. **Application Layer** - Input validation, SQL injection prevention, XSS protection
3. **Data Layer** - Encryption at rest (AES-256), encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
4. **Identity Layer** - MFA, biometric auth, SSO with OAuth 2.0
5. **Forensic Layer** - FanzForensics™ signatures on all content

### Compliance
- **2257 & 2258** - Age verification records stored in FanzHubVault
- **GDPR** - Right to access, delete, portability
- **CCPA** - California privacy rights
- **SOC 2 Type II** - Security, availability, confidentiality
- **PCI-DSS** - Payment card security
