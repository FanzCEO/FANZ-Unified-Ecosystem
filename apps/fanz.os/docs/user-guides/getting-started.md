## Account Creation

### Registration Options

FansLab offers multiple ways to create your account:

**Social Sign-Up:**
- Google Account
- Facebook Account
- Twitter Account
- Instagram Account
- Reddit Account

**Email Registration:**
- Enter your email address
- Create a secure password
- Verify your email address

**SMS Registration:**
- Enter your phone number
- Receive verification code
- Complete phone verification

### Account Verification

After creating your account:

1. **Email Verification**: Click the link in your verification email
2. **Phone Verification**: Enter the SMS code (if using phone registration)
3. **Identity Verification**: Upload a government-issued ID for creator accounts

## Profile Setup

### Basic Information

Complete your profile with:

- **Profile Picture**: Upload a clear, recent photo
- **Display Name**: Choose how others will see you
- **Bio/Description**: Tell others about yourself
- **Location**: Optional location information
- **Website/Social Links**: Link your other platforms

### Privacy Settings

Configure your privacy preferences:

- **Profile Visibility**: Public, followers-only, or private
- **Message Settings**: Who can send you messages
- **Notification Preferences**: Email and push notifications
- **Search Visibility**: Whether you appear in search results

## Age Verification

**Important**: FansLab is an adult platform requiring users to be 18+ years old.

### Verification Process

1. **Age Declaration**: Confirm you are 18 or older
2. **ID Upload**: Upload government-issued photo ID
3. **Document Review**: Our team reviews your submission (24-48 hours)
4. **Verification Confirmation**: Receive email confirmation

### Accepted Documents

- Driver's License
- Passport
- State/National ID Card
- Military ID

**Note**: Documents must be current, clear, and show your full name and date of birth.

## Choosing Your Role

### Creator Account

Choose this if you want to:
- Upload and sell content
- Build a subscriber base
- Earn money from tips and subscriptions
- Go live and interact with fans
- Access creator analytics

**Requirements:**
- Age verification (18+)
- Tax information (for payouts)
- Banking details for payments

### Fan Account

Choose this if you want to:
- Browse and discover content
- Subscribe to creators
- Purchase pay-per-view content
- Send tips and messages
- Interact with live streams

**Requirements:**
- Age verification (18+)
- Payment method for purchases

### Admin Account

Administrative accounts are invitation-only for platform management.

## First Steps by Role

### For New Creators

1. **Complete Verification**: Upload ID and tax documents

4. **Set Subscription Price**: Choose your monthly subscription rate

### For New Fans

1. **Complete Age Verification**: Upload required ID
2. **Add Payment Method**: Credit card or cryptocurrency
3. **Browse Content**: Use the discover page to find creators
4. **Follow Creators**: Build your personalized feed
5. **Try Free Content**: Explore free posts to find creators you like
6. **Consider Subscriptions**: Subscribe to your favorite creators

## Payment Setup

### For Fans (Making Purchases)

**Accepted Payment Methods:**
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- PayPal
- Cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.)
- Bank Transfer (select regions)

**Setting Up Payments:**
1. Go to Settings → Payment Methods
2. Add your preferred payment method
3. Verify payment method (small charge may apply)
4. Set default payment method

### For Creators (Receiving Payments)

**Payout Methods:**
- Bank Transfer (ACH/Wire)
- PayPal
- Cryptocurrency wallets
- Digital payment platforms

**Setting Up Payouts:**
1. Go to Creator Dashboard → Earnings
2. Add payout method
3. Verify banking information
4. Set payout frequency (daily, weekly, or monthly)

### Payment Security

- All payments are processed through certified payment processors
- SSL encryption protects all transactions
- PCI DSS compliant payment handling
- Fraud detection and prevention systems

## Security Features

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

**Setting up 2FA:**
1. Go to Settings → Security
2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication
3. Download an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy)
4. Scan QR code or enter setup key
5. Enter verification code to confirm

### Account Security Best Practices

- Use a strong, unique password
- Enable 2FA on your account
- Regularly review login activity
- Don't share account credentials
- Log out on shared devices
- Keep contact information updated

### Privacy Controls

- Control who can message you
- Manage blocked users
- Set content visibility preferences
- Control search appearance
- Manage data sharing settings
