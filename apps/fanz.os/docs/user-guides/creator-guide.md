## Content Creation & Management

### Content Types

**Photos:**
- High-resolution images (minimum 1080p recommended)
- Multiple photos in a single post
- Photo editing tools built-in
- Watermarking for protection

**Videos:**
- Short-form videos (up to 15 minutes)
- Long-form content (up to 60 minutes for premium creators)
- Video editing suite with filters and effects
- Thumbnail customization

**Live Streams:**
- Real-time interaction with fans
- Screen sharing capabilities
- Multi-camera support
- Recording for later viewing

**Text Posts:**
- Rich text formatting
- Polls and interactive elements
- Link sharing (approved domains)
- Scheduled posting

### Content Upload Process

1. **Select Content Type**: Choose photo, video, or text post
2. **Upload Media**: Drag and drop or browse files
3. **Add Description**: Write engaging captions with hashtags
4. **Set Visibility**:
   - Free (visible to all followers)
   - Subscription-only (paid subscribers only)
   - Pay-per-view (individual purchase required)
5. **Price Setting**: Set PPV prices if applicable
6. **Schedule**: Post now or schedule for later
7. **Advanced Options**: Location tags, collaboration tags, content warnings

### Content Optimization

**Best Practices:**
- Post consistently (daily recommended)
- Use high-quality visuals
- Engage with comments promptly
- Create series or themed content
- Cross-promote on other platforms

**SEO Optimization:**
- Use relevant hashtags (#lifestyle, #fitness, etc.)
- Write descriptive captions
- Include location information when relevant
- Use trending keywords
- Create searchable content titles

### Content Management Tools

**Organization:**
- Create content folders and categories

- Bulk operations (delete, move, edit)
- Content calendar view
- Archive old content

**Performance Tracking:**
- View metrics for each post

- Monitor subscriber growth from posts
- Revenue attribution by content

## Compliance & Safety

### Content Guidelines

**Acceptable Content:**
- Original content you own rights to
- Content featuring consenting adults (18+)
- Properly attributed collaborative content
- Content that complies with platform terms

**Prohibited Content:**
- Content involving minors
- Non-consensual content
- Violence or illegal activities
- Copyrighted material without permission
- Spam or misleading content

### Legal Compliance

**Required Documentation:**
- 2257 compliance records for adult content
- Model releases for collaborative content
- Age verification for all participants
- Rights documentation for music/media
- Tax documentation for earnings

**Best Practices:**
- Keep detailed records of all content
- Obtain proper consent forms
- Verify age of all participants
- Understand local laws and regulations
- Consult legal counsel when needed

### Safety Measures

**Personal Safety:**
- Never share personal information (address, real name)
- Use platform messaging systems only
- Be cautious of fan meetings
- Trust your instincts about interactions
- Report suspicious or threatening behavior

**Digital Security:**
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Regularly review account access
- Be cautious of phishing attempts
- Keep software and apps updated

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

**Upload Problems:**
- **Issue**: Video won't upload
- **Solution**: Check file size (max 2GB), format (MP4 recommended), and internet connection

**Payment Issues:**
- **Issue**: Payout not received
- **Solution**: Verify banking information, check payout threshold, contact support if needed

**Subscriber Issues:**
- **Issue**: Subscriber count dropping
- **Solution**: Review recent content quality, check for pricing changes, engage more actively

**Technical Problems:**
- **Issue**: Live stream won't start
- **Solution**: Check internet connection, browser permissions, restart device

### Getting Support

**Self-Help Resources:**
1. Check FAQ section
2. Use AI assistant for quick answers
3. Browse community forums
4. Watch tutorial videos
5. Read feature documentation

**Contact Support:**
- Use support ticket system for complex issues
- Provide detailed information about problems
- Include screenshots or videos when helpful
- Be patient for response (24-48 hours typical)
- Escalate urgent issues through priority support
