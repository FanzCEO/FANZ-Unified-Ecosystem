## Account & Registration

### How do I create an account?
You can sign up using:
- Social accounts (Google, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Reddit)
- Email address with password
- Phone number with SMS verification

All users must verify they are 18+ years old with government-issued ID.

### Why do I need to verify my age?
FansLab is an adult platform that requires all users to be 18 or older. This is both for legal compliance and to ensure a safe environment for all users.

### What documents are accepted for age verification?
We accept:
- Driver's License
- Passport  
- State/National ID Card
- Military ID

Documents must be current, clear, and show your full name and date of birth.

### How long does verification take?
Age verification typically takes 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email confirmation once approved. During peak times, it may take up to 72 hours.

### Can I change my username after registration?
Yes, you can change your username in Account Settings. However, usernames can only be changed once every 30 days, and your previous username will be unavailable for 60 days.

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your registered email
4. Check your email for reset instructions
5. Follow the link to create a new password

## Subscriptions & Payments

### What payment methods do you accept?
**For Fans:**
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- PayPal
- Cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum, others)
- Apple Pay and Google Pay
- Bank Transfer (select regions)

**For Creator Payouts:**
- Bank Transfer (ACH/Wire)
- PayPal
- Cryptocurrency wallets
- Digital payment platforms

### How much does a subscription cost?
Subscription prices are set by individual creators and typically range from $4.99 to $50+ per month. Many creators offer promotional rates for new subscribers.

### Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel any subscription at any time. You'll retain access to subscriber content until the end of your current billing period.

### What happens to my content access when I cancel?
- You keep access until your current billing period ends
- You can no longer view new subscriber-only content
- Previously purchased PPV content remains accessible
- You can resubscribe anytime to regain access

### Are there any hidden fees?
No hidden fees. You only pay:
- Subscription prices set by creators
- PPV content prices
- Tips you choose to send
- Processing fees may apply for certain payment methods

### How do refunds work?
Refunds are handled case-by-case:
- Technical issues: Usually eligible for full refund
- Content disputes: Reviewed based on platform guidelines
- Accidental purchases: Refundable within 24 hours
- Subscription refunds: Pro-rated based on usage

## Technical Issues

### Why won't videos play?
Common solutions:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try refreshing the page
3. Clear browser cache and cookies
4. Try a different browser
5. Ensure your browser supports HTML5 video
6. Disable ad blockers temporarily

### The app keeps crashing on my phone
Try these fixes:
1. Force close and restart the app
2. Update to the latest app version
3. Restart your phone
4. Clear app cache (Android)
5. Reinstall the app
6. Check available storage space

### I can't upload content
For upload issues:
1. Check file size limits (2GB max for videos)
2. Verify supported file formats
3. Test your internet connection speed
4. Try uploading at a different time
5. Compress large files before uploading
6. Use a wired internet connection if possible

### Live streams won't start
Troubleshooting steps:
1. Check internet speed (5+ Mbps upload required)
2. Allow camera and microphone permissions
3. Close other programs using camera/microphone
4. Try a different browser
5. Test with a different device
6. Check firewall and antivirus settings

### I'm having login problems
Solutions for login issues:
1. Check username and password spelling
2. Try password reset if needed
3. Clear browser cache and cookies
4. Disable VPN if using one
5. Try incognito/private browsing mode
6. Contact support for account lockouts

2. Click the three dots menu
3. Select "Block User"
4. Confirm the action

To report a user:
1. Click "Report" on their profile or content
2. Select the reason for reporting
3. Provide additional details
4. Submit the report

Yes, we strongly recommend enabling 2FA:
1. Go to Account Settings > Security

3. Download an authenticator app
