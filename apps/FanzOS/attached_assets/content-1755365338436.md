# Compliance Guide for Starz and Fanz

**Fanz™ Unlimited Network (FUN) L.L.C. Official Legal Compliance Handbook**

It ensures full compliance with U.S., EU, and international regulations while protecting the rights of Fanz members and Content Stars.**

**SECTION 1: USER AGREEMENTS & CONTRACTS**

**1.1 Fanz™ Unlimited Network (FUN) L.L.C. User Agreement**

**This Agreement is legally binding under:**

- **Contract Law of the State of Wyoming, United States**

- **Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act (E-SIGN Act) (15 U.S.C. Chapter 96)**

- **Uniform Electronic Transactions Act (UETA)**


**User Responsibilities**

**Users must:**

- **Provide accurate information during registration.**

- **Adhere to content and behavior policies set forth by FUN.**

- **Understand that violation of terms can result in immediate suspension or legal action.**


**Dispute Resolution & Arbitration**

- **Mandatory Arbitration Clause: Users waive their right to sue in public court and must resolve disputes via arbitration.**

- **Class Action Waiver: Users cannot file class-action lawsuits against FUN.**

- **Governing Jurisdiction: All arbitration will take place in Sheridan, Wyoming, USA.**


* * *

**SECTION 2: CONTENT MODERATION & ENFORCEMENT**

**2.1 Prohibited Content Policy**

**Illegal & Restricted Content Categories The following content is strictly prohibited on any FUN platform:**

- **Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) – Violation of 18 U.S.C. § 2252A (Federal Law).**

- **Bestiality – Violation of U.S. Animal Crush Video Prohibition Act, 18 U.S.C. § 48.**

- **Revenge Porn / Non-Consensual Intimate Media – Violation of 47 U.S.C. § 230 (SAFE SEX Act).**

- **Human Trafficking / Exploitation – Violation of Trafficking Victims Protection Act (TVPA), 22 U.S.C. § 7102.**


**2.2 DMCA Copyright Infringement Takedown Policy**

**Legal Basis for Copyright Enforcement**

- **Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA), 17 U.S.C. § 512**

- **Berne Convention for the Protection of Literary and Artistic Works**


**DMCA Takedown Process**

**1.** **Receipt of Complaint – Must include signature, copyrighted work identification, location of infringing material, and legal statement of good faith.**

**2.** **Content Removal – FUN must disable access within 24 hours of a valid claim.**

**3.** **User Notification & Counter-Notification Process – Users have 10 days to respond before content is permanently removed.**

* * *

**SECTION 3: DATA RETENTION, DELETION & SECURITY COMPLIANCE**

**3.1 Data Retention & Deletion Policy**

|     |     |     |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Data Type** | **Retention Period** | **Legal Reference** |
| **User Agreements** | **5 years after termination** | **GDPR, CCPA** |
| **Billing Records** | **7 years** | **IRS Tax Compliance** |
| **Removed Content (Forensic Records)** | **10 years** | **Law Enforcement Requirement** |

**3.2 Legal Hold & Investigation Protocol**

- **Legal Holds Prevent Data Deletion During Investigations.**

- **Only the Legal Team Can Remove a Hold.**


**3.3 Retention & Deletion Email Notifications**

- **1-Year Retention Review Notification: Users are informed about data retention policies.**

- **Final Deletion Notice (30-Day Warning): Users are notified before their data is permanently deleted.**

- **Legal Hold Notification: Users are informed if their data is under legal hold and cannot be deleted.**
