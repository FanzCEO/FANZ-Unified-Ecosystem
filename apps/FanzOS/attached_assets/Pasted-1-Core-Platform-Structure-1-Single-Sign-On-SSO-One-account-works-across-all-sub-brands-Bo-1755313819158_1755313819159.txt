1. Core Platform & Structure
	1.	Single Sign-On (SSO)
	•	One account works across all sub-brands (BoyFanz, GirlFanz, PupFanz, etc.).
	•	OAuth2/JWT tokens ensure seamless navigation.
	•	Role-based access (Fan, Creator, Moderator, Admin).
	2.	Multi-Tenant Architecture
	•	Each sub-platform has unique branding, logos, and themes.
	•	Separate landing pages/domains but shared authentication and wallet.
	•	Admin toggles to enable/disable modules per tenant.
	3.	Unified Homepage / Portal
	•	FUN-branded portal directing users to:
	•	VIP area (ViserTube / FanzClips).
	•	Forums & community.
	•	Marketplace (influencer/freelance).
	•	Events & bookings.
	•	Virtual cards & wallet.
