## How to Use the AI Assistant

### Accessing the AI Assistant

The AI Assistant is available throughout the platform:
- **Chat Icon**: Click the chat bubble icon in the bottom right
- **Help Menu**: Access through Settings > Help & Support > AI Assistant
- **Mobile App**: Swipe up from the bottom or tap the help icon
- **Voice Commands**: Say "Hey FansLab" (when enabled)

### Types of Questions You Can Ask

**Account & Setup:**
- "How do I verify my age?"
- "What payment methods can I use?"
- "How do I change my subscription price?"
- "Help me set up two-factor authentication"

**Content & Features:**
- "How do I upload a video?"
- "What's the difference between PPV and subscription content?"
- "How do I start a live stream?"
- "Show me how to use the content scheduler"

**Earnings & Analytics:**
- "How do I check my earnings?"
- "When will I get paid?"
- "What does my analytics dashboard mean?"
- "Help me understand my subscriber growth"

**Technical Issues:**
- "My video won't upload"
- "I can't log into my account"
- "The app keeps crashing"
- "Live stream won't start"

### Getting Better Answers

**Be Specific:**
- Instead of: "Help with payments"
- Try: "How do I add a new credit card for subscriptions?"

**Include Context:**
- "I'm a creator trying to set up live streaming"
- "As a fan, I can't access content I paid for"
- "I'm getting an error message that says..."

**Ask Follow-up Questions:**
- "Can you explain that in more detail?"
- "What if that doesn't work?"
- "Show me step-by-step instructions"

## Feature-Specific Help

**Upload Help:**
```
AI: I can help you upload content! What type of content are you uploading?
User: A video
AI: Great! Here's what you need to know about video uploads:
- Maximum file size: 2GB
- Supported formats: MP4, MOV, AVI
- Recommended resolution: 1080p or higher
- Would you like me to walk you through the upload process?
```

**Pricing Guidance:**

```
User: Help me price my subscription
AI: I'll help you set a competitive subscription price. First, let me ask:

- How often do you post?
- Are you new to the platform or established?
Based on your answers, I'll suggest optimal pricing strategies.
```

```

```

## Troubleshooting with AI

### Systematic Problem Solving

The AI Assistant uses a systematic approach to solve problems:

1. **Identify the Issue**: Understanding exactly what's wrong
2. **Gather Information**: Ask relevant questions about your setup
3. **Provide Solutions**: Offer step-by-step fixes
4. **Verify Resolution**: Confirm the problem is solved
5. **Escalate if Needed**: Connect you with human support

### Common Issue Resolution

**Upload Problems:**
```
AI: I see you're having upload issues. Let me help diagnose this:
1. What type of file are you uploading?
2. What's the file size?
3. What error message do you see?
4. What browser are you using?
Based on your answers, I'll provide targeted solutions.
```

**Payment Issues:**
```
AI: Payment problems can be frustrating. Let's identify the issue:
1. Are you trying to make a purchase or having payout issues?
2. What payment method are you using?
3. What error message appears?
4. When did this problem start?
I'll guide you through the appropriate resolution steps.
```

## AI Assistant Capabilities

### What the AI Can Do

**Instant Help:**
- Answer questions about any platform feature
- Provide step-by-step instructions
- Explain error messages and solutions
- Guide you through complex processes
- Offer best practice recommendations

**Personalized Assistance:**
- Remember your account type (creator/fan)
- Adapt answers to your experience level
- Provide relevant examples and scenarios
- Suggest features you might not know about
- Learn from your previous questions

**Multi-Modal Support:**
- Text-based chat interface
- Voice commands and responses
- Screen sharing for complex issues
- Image analysis for error screenshots
- Video tutorials when helpful

### What the AI Cannot Do

**Limitations:**
- Cannot access your private account data
- Cannot make changes to your account settings
- Cannot process payments or refunds
- Cannot access other users' private information
- Cannot override platform policies or terms

**When to Contact Human Support:**
- Complex billing disputes
- Legal or compliance issues
- Account suspension appeals
- Security incidents
- Bug reports requiring investigation
