### Age Verification and Identity Compliance

#### US Age Verification Requirements

Age verification has become a critical compliance requirement for adult content platforms. As of June 2025, 18 U.S. states have passed age verification laws for adult content, with six scheduled to be enacted within the year and sixteen being introduced into state legislatures <a class="reference" href="https://designerup.co/blog/i-studied-the-ux-ui-of-over-200-onboarding-flows-heres-everything-i-learned/" target="_blank">1</a>. These laws mandate that platforms verify users are at least 18 years old before allowing access to adult content.

**Attribute-Based Verification Systems**

Attribute-based verification systems represent the most secure and privacy-preserving approach to age verification. These systems verify only necessary information (such as being over 18) without exposing full personal identifiers like names, addresses, or government ID numbers <a class="reference" href="https://designerup.co/blog/i-studied-the-ux-ui-of-over-200-onboarding-flows-heres-everything-i-learned/" target="_blank">1</a>. This approach balances regulatory compliance with user privacy protection, a critical consideration for adult content platforms where discretion is paramount.

**Recommended Implementation:**

For CougarFanz, a comprehensive age verification system should include:

- **Creator Verification:** Government-issued ID verification with facial recognition matching for all content creators, ensuring compliance with platform policies and legal requirements
- **Subscriber Verification:** Age-gate with attribute-based verification using third-party services that confirm age without storing sensitive personal information
- **Document Retention:** Secure storage of verification records for minimum statutory periods (typically 7 years) with encrypted databases
- **Regular Re-verification:** Periodic re-verification of creator identities (annually) to maintain compliance and detect fraudulent accounts

#### International Age Verification Standards

Beyond US requirements, international markets impose varying age verification standards. The European Union's Digital Services Act and UK's Online Safety Act establish frameworks requiring platforms to implement age-appropriate design and verification mechanisms. CougarFanz must implement geo-location detection and jurisdiction-specific verification protocols to ensure compliance across operating markets.

### Data Privacy and Protection Regulations

#### GDPR Compliance (European Union)

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) establishes comprehensive data protection requirements for platforms serving EU residents. Key compliance requirements include:

**Data Processing Principles:**
- **Lawful Basis:** Establish legitimate legal basis for data processing (typically consent or contractual necessity)
- **Data Minimization:** Collect only data necessary for platform operations
- **Purpose Limitation:** Use data only for stated purposes
- **Storage Limitation:** Retain data only as long as necessary
- **Security:** Implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures

**User Rights:**
- **Right to Access:** Provide users with copies of their personal data within 30 days of request
- **Right to Rectification:** Allow users to correct inaccurate personal information
- **Right to Erasure:** Delete user data upon request (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Right to Data Portability:** Provide user data in machine-readable format for transfer to other platforms
- **Right to Object:** Allow users to opt out of certain data processing activities

**Consent Requirements:**
- Explicit, informed consent for data collection and processing
- Separate consent for different processing activities (e.g., marketing communications)
- Easy withdrawal of consent at any time
- Age-appropriate consent mechanisms (parental consent for users under 16)

**Data Breach Notification:**
- Report data breaches to supervisory authorities within 72 hours
- Notify affected users when breach poses high risk to their rights
- Maintain detailed breach response procedures and documentation

#### CCPA/CPRA Compliance (California)

The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and its successor, the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), establish data protection requirements for platforms serving California residents:

**Consumer Rights:**
- **Right to Know:** Disclose categories and specific pieces of personal information collected
- **Right to Delete:** Delete personal information upon request (subject to exceptions)
- **Right to Opt-Out:** Allow users to opt out of personal information sales
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** Prohibit discrimination against users exercising privacy rights

**Business Obligations:**
- Provide clear privacy notices describing data collection and use practices
- Implement "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" mechanisms
- Respond to consumer requests within 45 days
- Maintain records of consumer requests and responses

#### Additional Privacy Regulations

- **Brazil's LGPD:** Similar requirements to GDPR for Brazilian users
- **Canada's PIPEDA:** Privacy requirements for Canadian users
