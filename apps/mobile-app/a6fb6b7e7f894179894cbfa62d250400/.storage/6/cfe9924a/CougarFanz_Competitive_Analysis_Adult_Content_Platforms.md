# CougarFanz Competitive Intelligence: Adult Content Platform Market Analysis

## Executive Summary

The adult content creator platform market is dominated by OnlyFans, with over 2.1 million creators and 190 million active users as of 2025, generating $7.2 billion in gross revenue in 2024 [ref: 1-3]. However, the market shows significant gaps in serving older women creators (35-55+), presenting a clear opportunity for CougarFanz. While platforms like OnlyFans, Fansly, and Fanvue serve creators across all demographics, no major platform specifically targets or optimizes for mature women creators, leaving this niche underserved [ref: 1-0].

## Market Size and Growth Trends

### Overall Adult Content Platform Market

The global digital adult content market was valued at USD 6,980 million in 2024 and is projected to reach USD 14,363.3 million in 2025, with expectations to grow to USD 7,454.64 million by 2033, reflecting a steady 6.8% growth rate [ref: 2-0]. The market has experienced explosive growth, with OnlyFans alone seeing user base increases of 1,291.85% between 2019 and 2021 [ref: 4-1].

### Creator Economy Scale

OnlyFans creators collectively earned $6.6 billion in 2023, with the platform paying out $12.5 billion in gross merchandise volume in 2021 [ref: 4-1]. However, income distribution is highly concentrated: the top 1% of creators earn 33% of total platform revenue, while the top 10% earn 73% of all income [ref: 4-0]. The median OnlyFans account makes approximately $180 per month ($2,160 annually), though 300+ creators earn over $1 million per year [ref: 4-1].

### Market Gaps for Older Women Creators

Research indicates that older adults (50+) represent a growing market segment, with 74% of adults ages 50+ reporting regular social media use [ref: 3-0]. Despite this, platforms have not specifically tailored their offerings to mature women creators. The emergence of "granfluencers" (influencers aged 60+) demonstrates demand for older adult content creators, with some achieving millions of followers and significant engagement from younger audiences [ref: 3-1].
